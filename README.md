# BRICS
In this study, we developed BRICS (Biochemical Recurrence rIsk Classification Score) , a multimodal BCR risk score that integrates a Multi-scale WSI pathology model with clinical variables. Our work represents the largest international multicenter effort, involving 4090 patients from six independent cohorts. After rigorous quality control we analysed 4368 WSIs, extracting more than 4·5 million non-overlapping tiles at 10×, 20×, and 40× and training a Multi-scale Representation Attention Multiple-Instance Learning (MSRAMIL) framework with positional-spatial encoding (PSIG) and a pseudo-bag attention strategy. BRICS outperformed CAPRA-S across all cohorts, and was capable of reclassifying CAPRA-S intermediate-risk patients into distinct high-risk and low-risk groups with distinct different recurrence outcomes. Furthermore, we developed BRICS system with one-step real-time analysis of WSIs and clinical data to assist urologists in patients’ management.
